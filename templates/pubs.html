Top Tips for Clubbing

Here's some advice to ensure you have a good night out:

  • Pre-drink at home to save some money. Drinks can be expensive in clubs!
  • Know your limits. Don’t buy more drinks if you feel you don’t need it. You can always ask for water instead.
  • Agree with a friend to go home together so you don’t end up walking home on your own. If you do end up alone, order a taxi instead of walking on your own.
  • At the end of the night, make sure to eat some food and drink lots of water

Staying Safe

Staying Safe

Newcastle University Safe Taxi Scheme.

If you have no money to get home but have your student card, you can call 0191 298 5050 and quote reference NEW02 to get a taxi home.

If you are in an emergency, call 999 for assistance.
